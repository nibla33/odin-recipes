Pizza

The humble pizza. Some call it a warm sandwich, others call it art. I will tell you how to make it with pineapples.

Ingredients

  • Pineapple canned
  • Tipo OO Flour
  • Yeast
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Whatever else your little heart desires

Instructions

  1. Make the dough and let it rise
  2. Cut up your toppings and prepare cheese
  3. Roll out the dough in a pizza shape
  4. Add the sauce ingredients and cheese
  5. Put the dough in a pizza oven
  6. Take it out and eat it
