Lasagna

The lasagna, a beautiful italian dish. This was passed down to me from the stomach as my mom ate it.

Ingredients

  • Crushed canned tomatoes
  • Milk
  • Minced meat
  • Unions
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • A pinch of flour
  • Celery
  • Carrots

Instructions

Lasagna is tasty because it is hard to make. There are many steps involved. If you are too weak to make it. Go to a restaurant instead.

  1. Make a bechamel sauce and grate cheese into it.
  2. Finely cut unions, celery and carrots.
  3. Cook the carrots celery and union
  4. Fry up some meat
  5. Cook the fried vegetables with the meat and tomato sauce Optional: Add tomato paste
  6. Layer the stuff and cook the lasagna until golden brown.
